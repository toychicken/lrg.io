(That's 11 Trillion dollars for those that need it)

If you want a point of reference that kind of money could:

* Run the NHS for about 60 years
* Move Tuvalu's annual GDP from the bottom of the table to #3 behind the US and China (for a year at least)
* Buy 28,128,924 average UK houses (at approx Â£300k / approx $409k), enough for about half of the UK adult population, or about 10% of US population (funnily enough avg house price there is about the same)
* Give everyone on earth about $1100.
