The purpose of this page is to try and find a new way to show images in a blog. Something a little different from the usual static image, or animated GIF.

I was reminded of miniature paper theatre sets that my grandmother and mother used to make. An isometric view of the world, that gave a sense of depth for the actors to move around on the stage.
